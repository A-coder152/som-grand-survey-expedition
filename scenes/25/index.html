Beyond the snow,
Above the mountain,
There's a sight to behold!

Legend has it that there exists a great island called Cantada. There is even a rumour of hiker who travelled great lengths, treking across dozens of islands before discovering the snowy mountain. It is believed at the hill top, the hiker shouted "it can, tada!" when they saw magical lights suddenly emerge from the dark...


Unfortunately, many have attempted to see the hidden gem, but the snowy conditions have misguided all to a dead end. It is believed only those chosen are lucky enough to see the land, Cantada.
