However, while digging for these precious minerals, pheusians found pieces of wood, concrete, and red bricks. These types of objects are near impossible to naturally end up deep in a ravine. A HUGE TREX, chained together, roaring so loud the workers eardrums were on the brink of explosion! They tried to run, but the way they came from got closed off due to rubble and rock :0. The workers were trapped, in a room where the TREX could reach his head in any corner, since its head was unchained. The tools that the workers had were completely eaten by the TREX, and so they had to go to the very edge of the room, and dig with bare hands. Many couldn't make it, but those who escaped had beat a great feat in Pheuskind.
