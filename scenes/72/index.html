The Call of Glub!

Once upon a time, there flowed a mysterious river that allied the mysterious Sea of Glub with the mystical Glacier of Elephant of Elephant Butt. The river was no ordinary stream; it flowed beneath the in the isle of SOM with a mystical glow, and every wave of it made a curious glubbing sound as if the waters carried some monsters. The Young Coders who passed by its banks made them feel curious about the glubbing noise and decided to uncover its mystery
